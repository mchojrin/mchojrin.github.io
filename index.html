Hello! Welcome to my blog

I am Mauro Chojrin, technical coach for software development teams

In case you're wondering, the goal of a technical coaching engagement is to help software development teams reach greater levels of excellence.

More specifically, I help software development teams:

  • Reduce the number of bugs that reach production
  • Reduce the time it takes to move from the PO's head to the users hands
  • Reduce the bus factor
  • Reduce software maintenance costs

I do so by putting together hands-on workshops, sharing theoretical materials, participating in the teams day-to-day and working closely with the team leadership

In this space I'll be sharing some learnings gathered while working with software development teams as a Technical Coach.

I hope you find these essays useful.
